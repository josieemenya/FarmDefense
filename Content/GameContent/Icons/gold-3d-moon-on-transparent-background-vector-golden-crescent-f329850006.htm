Product description

Photography

Illustrations and photographs are printed on premium glossy photo paper with a weight of 250 g/m². Its smooth, glossy surface provides excellent clarity and sharpness, enhancing contrast and colour saturation, making it suitable for high-resolution photographs and images. The glossy surface has the advantage of being less susceptible to touch marks.

We offer many standard sizes, which you can also personalise to better combine with others. In addition to the standard sizes, we also offer perfectly fitting frames – to make your image complete!

High-quality handmade production and fast shipping

Quality always comes first
The meticulous handmade production of every print in the Czech Republic is not only our pride, but also the best calling card that keeps our customers coming back to us. We treat each print individually, always check its quality and package it securely so that it will not be damaged during its journey to you.

We print the same day!
You order and we start printing the same day. We ship all types of prints within 48 hours from ordering.

TIP: Add a white or black border to the image

If you want your chosen motif to look lighter on the wall, you can have the image printed with a white or black border.

The border works as a border and visually frames the image around its perimeter. It is automatically calculated for each dimension you choose.

You won't go wrong with a "passepartout" and your images will look professional - plus the border looks perfect in the frame!
